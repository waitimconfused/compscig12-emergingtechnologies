Extended Reality

What is Extended Reality

Extended Reality covers all immersive technologies (VR, AR, MR) that allow people to engage with digital content through the combination of physical and digital worlds.

Impact on Developing Countries

In developing countries, XR technology can be used to increase access to education and professional training through the development of virtual classrooms. This will enable people tolearn skills such as medicine, engineering, and technology despite the lack of such equipment.
