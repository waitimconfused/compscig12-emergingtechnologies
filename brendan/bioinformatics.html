Bioinformatics

What is Bioinformatics?

Bioinformatics involves the use of computers, software tools, and databases to generate large data sets that utilize genomics and proteomics.

Impact on a Minority in Canada

This technology can benefit minority groups in Canada, such as Indigenous Peoples with rare genetic conditions, by assisting scientists in studying their information to determine possible cures. Scientists can study large sets of biological information to better understand health conditions in other small groups of society.
