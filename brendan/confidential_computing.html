Confidential Computing

What is Confidential Computing?

Confidential computing is the protection of data using cloud technology to control encryption keys and isolate sensitive data in a protected CPU.

Impact on the World Economy

This technology has the ability to affect the world economy by helping companies and financial institutions handle sensitive information securely through the cloud. This means that various companies across the world will be able to collaborate safely.
