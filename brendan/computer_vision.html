Computer Vision

What is Computer Vision?

Computer vision is a subfield of AI that uses machine learning to analyze, process, and interpret visual data like images and videos.

Impact on Majority of Canadian Society

In the Canadian society, this technology is commonly used in security cameras, traffic management systems, as well as retail systems such as automated checkouts. While the systems have enhanced efficiency and safety, there have also been concerns over the issue of privacy due to the collection of information from the population.
